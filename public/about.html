Frequently Asked Questions

EcoRadarPH primarily supports the Central Luzon and NCR regions of the Philippines. However, the app should function in other locations by utilizing your device's location data. Please note that in such cases, you may encounter issues like the inability to display your city's name accurately.

The air quality forecast evaluates key air pollutants, including:

  • Particulate Matter PM10: Larger particles in the air, such as dust or pollen.
  • Particulate Matter PM2.5: Fine particles that can penetrate deep into the lungs.
  • Nitrogen Dioxide (NO₂): A harmful gas often emitted by vehicles and industrial processes.
  • Ozone (O₃): A gas formed when sunlight reacts with pollutants.
These pollutants are measured using the United States Air Quality Index (AQI), which categorizes air quality levels from Good to Hazardous.Refer to the table below for more details about the AQI levels and their health implications.
